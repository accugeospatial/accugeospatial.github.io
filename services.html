Special Programs

Spatial Thinking Campaign - STC

Being a people centred organisation, we believe that technology is a significant player that can catalyse Africa’s development. We have therefore as part of our CSR instituted the ‘STC’ among students in second-cycle institutions. This program equips students with spatial thinking skills. The program involves spending a day with the institution and taking lessons from data collection to digital map creation and analytics. For each institution, ACCU works with the students to implement training ie solution developers, campus digital maps etc hosted on school’s websites.

Accu Added Advantage Program – 3AP

At ACCU, we believe in sustainable implementation within the scarce resources of the client. We therefore have jump-start packages to meet client specification to ensure the implementation of GIS in their organisation. 3AP focusses on Jump Start kit module for existing users of Esri and Trimble solutions leveraging on existing client resources.
