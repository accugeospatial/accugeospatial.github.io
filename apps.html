Ghana Health Facility Locator (Version 1.0)

Use this application to search and find all health facilities in Ghana. It contains a list of all major and regional hospitals, their directions from the user's location which you can share to anyone.
The application combines the power of the major web mapping platforms that are used by most professionals all over the world. The application has an attractive and interactive interface that makes it's usage useful and fun.
The application works partly online and offline. However, it is light in weight and so can be quick to install.
